Career Counselling

Why everyone needs a Career Coach

The key to having a satisfying and brilliant career is knowing yourself. Knowing and using your strengths, understanding your values and aligning those to what you’re passionate about.

If you’re thinking of finding a new job we will help you do that successfully.

Or perhaps you are considering a complete career change, but don’t know where to begin. We will work together on the clarity and confidence you need to identify your ideal job and help you plan to make that a reality.

Perhaps you want to further your career where you are. Learn to become more effective, grow your responsibilities and raise your profile,

Career Change Programme

Unhappy in your present job? But unsure whether it’s the job, the company, the sector or all three that’s the problem? Maybe you know you’re in the wrong career for you, but you don’t know what other options are open to you.

Then you’ll love our Career Changers Programme.

In three months, we’ll get you from not having the slightest idea where to start to creating a vision that’s perfect for you. And giving you an action plan to help you get there.

There’s no need to battle away on your own, and fret about whether you’re doing the right thing. Enjoy the support of a coach, and get the results you’re after, fast!

How does it work?

Well it’s simple really. There are three stages.

EXPLORE: First we’ll get you to really know yourself. You’ll discover your key needs and values; your strengths and favourite skills; your interests and passions. They’re all the things that really float your boat, and will make your career deeply satisfying.

DREAM: Then we create a whole bunch of new career possibilities, and we’ll get really creative with this. You’ll get some great ideas you’d never thought of before. And we create a compelling vision for your whole life, not just your career.

DISCOVER: This is where you get even more focused about your chosen path, and look at the gaps that have to be bridged before you can get there. We then create a sound action plan to move you off in your new direction.

We’ll leave you with this quote from Mark Twain. ‘Twenty years from now you will be more disappointed by the things you didn’t do than by the ones you did. So throw off the bowlines. Sail away from the safe harbour. Catch the trade winds in your sails. Explore. Dream. Discover’
